[ 02 ] About

I like building things that feel inhabited: clear enough to use, specific enough to belong to someone, and strange enough to keep thinking about.

01 Start with behaviour.

A page can look beautiful in a screenshot and still be inert. The better question is what it does when someone comes back.

02 Make the invisible legible.

State, rhythm, constraints, and affordances should be felt through the interface before anyone needs them explained.

03 Keep the edge human.

Even technical work benefits from texture: good names, calm defaults, forgiving controls, and a little wit.
